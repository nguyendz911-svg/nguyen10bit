About Me

Every frame
is a choice.

I'm a cinematographer and Director of Photography based in Hanoi, Vietnam. I've spent over a decade working at the intersection of light, movement, and narrative — making images that do more than document.

Whether it's a TVC for a national brand, a music video for an independent artist, or a documentary about people the world hasn't noticed yet — my approach is the same: get close to the truth of the moment and let the camera follow.
